**Easily Launch Apps from Zen Browser with These Simple Steps:**

1. Copy the script to `~/.local/share/omarchy/bin/` on your system.
2. Update the keybindings in `hypr/bindings.conf`:

   * Use `omarchy-launch-webapp-zen` to launch apps.
   * Use `omarchy-launch-or-focus-webapp-zen` to focus on or launch apps.
